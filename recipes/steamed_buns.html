Steamed Buns

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 30g sugar
  • 5g yeast
  • 8g vegetable oil
  • 200g warm milk
  • 160g grated purple yam, cassava, or potato

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients except milk in a bowl.
  2. Begin kneading the mix in a stand mixer on the lowest setting, gradually pouring in milk. After milk is mixed, knead for 5 mins or until the dough is stretchy.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise until it doubles/triples in size. This may take around 1 hour in a proofing drawer or 2 hours at room temperature.
  4. Shape the dough, then roll it out into a log shape.
  5. Cut the log into arpund 20 small balls. Place them in steamer trays, cover them, and let them rise for another 1.5 hours.
  6. Pour about 1 inch of water into the bottom of the steamer, and heat it until steaming vigorously.
  7. Place the steamer trays with the buns in the steamer, and steam for 10-12 mins.
  8. Remove the buns and serve warm.
